10 years of Rails + 4.1 Launch Party.

Map Date/Time: 2014-04-26 11:00am Location: Doo - 402A, Town Square, Airport Road, Viman Nagar, Landmark: Above Dorabjee's, on the 4th Floor, Pune

In 2004 DHH announced the initial release for Rails. This year marks ten years since its release.

Let's celebrate this occasion by holding a launch party on 26th April 2014.

Rails 4.1 will be out at Railsconf this year. Considering that there have been major changes from 3x onwards to 4.0 - 4.1, we plan to do a small workshop, focusing on new features with 4.1.

The workshop will focus on new things like:

• Reviewing ActiveRecord concepts.

• Mailer Preview

• Message Verifier

• JSON Encoder changes

• Security changes like secrets.yml

• Spring/Binstubs etc
